# Privacy Policy for Bar Feeder Distance App

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-20

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Bar Feeder Distance mobile application (the "App").

**_Disclaimer:_** _This is a template and not legal advice. You should consult with a legal professional to ensure this policy is compliant with all applicable laws and regulations for your specific use case._

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

*   **Account Information:** To use the App, you must create an account using your company email address (`@edgetechnologies.com`) and a password. We collect your name and email address to create and manage your user account.
*   **User-Generated Content:** We collect the information you voluntarily submit through the App, which includes:
    *   New lathe and bar feeder models.
    *   Distance measurements, along with any related notes.
    *   Variations from standard measurements, including the reasons for the variation.

### Information We Collect Automatically

*   **Usage Information:** We may collect information about your interaction with the App, such as which features you use and the equipment you look up.
*   **Device Information:** We use local storage on your device to save your preferences, such as your chosen theme (light/dark mode) and your recent measurement lookups. This data is stored only on your device.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

*   To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality.
*   To create a central database of bar feeder and lathe setup distances for company use.
*   To associate submitted measurements and variations with a user for accountability and context.
*   To personalize your experience, for instance, by showing your recent lookups.
*   To communicate with you about your account or submissions if necessary.

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your information may be shared in the following circumstances:

*   **Within the Company:** Information you submit, such as measurements and variations, is accessible to other users within Edge Technologies to facilitate their work. Your name may be associated with your submissions.
*   **With Service Providers:** We use Supabase as our backend service provider for database hosting and authentication. Supabase is contractually obligated to protect your information and is not permitted to use it for any other purpose.
*   **For Legal Reasons:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request from a law enforcement or government agency.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in a Supabase database. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Your Choices

*   **Accessing and Updating Your Information:** You may review and update your account information within the App.
*   **Data Deletion:** If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please contact us. Note that some anonymized data related to measurements may be retained for business purposes.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by employees of Edge Technologies and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App or via email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
